IPTV - Tutorials

There are always many ways to get IPTV service on your device, but in this tutorial we will show you the best way to configure your device according to our server.

OTT IPTV STB Application

It's a preconfigured application. It was designed only for our server.

  1. Download the application on your android box.
    1. Copy this link: http://up.magnum.la/Android/IPTV-OTT-STALKER-V6.apk
    2. Paste it into a browser in your android box device. (Google Chrome Recommended)
    3. Press OK. (The application will be downloaded automatically)
  2. Open the application.
    1. Send to our support team the "MAC Address" displayed on the screen via our subscription form.
  3. Wait the response of our support team in 24 hours.
  4. Reboot the android box.

VLC Media Player

It's a media palyer software that works in streaming line with a m3u URL.

  1. Go to our subscription form, fill out the form and turn on the m3u URL in streaming line option then submit the form.
  2. Our support team will contact you in 24 hours to send you the m3u URL and the information you need.
  3. Download VLC media player on your windows device.
  4. Open the software.
    1. Click on Media in top left corner.
    2. Click on "Stream".
    3. Click on "Network".
    4. Add the m3u URL.
    5. Click on "Play".
