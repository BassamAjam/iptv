Devices Configurations

Download OTT IPTV STB Application

  1. Open this website page in a browser on your android box. (Google Chrome Recommended)
  2. Click here and the application will be automatically downloaded on your box.
  3. Open the application and send to our support team the "MAC Address" displayed on the screen.
  4. Our support team will contact you within 24 hours.

VLC Media Player

  1. Download VLC media player on your windows device.
  2. Open the software.
    1. Click on Media in top left corner.
    2. Click on "Stream".
    3. Click on "Network".
    4. Add the m3u link. (Given by our support team)
    5. Click on "Play".

IPTV - Smarters Player Application

IPTV - Smarters Pro Application
