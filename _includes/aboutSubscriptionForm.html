About The Subscription Form

Personal Information

The purpose of requesting personal information is to distinguish between our customers and not to make mistakes.

Device

There are many manners to get the IPTV service on your device and it's different between each one. So we always need to know on which device the customer want try our IPTV service to give him the best assistance.

MAC Address

A MAC address is a code that defines certain devices and applications. We usually use it to connect it to our IPTV server. There are some customers who already know how IPTV works. They can give the MAC address directly to our team to facilitate the process of obtaining a subscription.

Streaming Line

The streaming line is a link generated by our server. It's a different way to connect certain devices with our server. It can also be in the form of an account.

Subscription Period

After studying the best price we can offer to the customers, we decided as a team to give an option to subscribe with us for a period of 3 months, 6 months, 12 months or 24 months.
