Description Of The Fields

Personal Information

The purpose of requesting personal information is to distinguish between our customers and not to make mistakes.

Device

There are many ways to get IPTV service on your device and it's different between each one. So we need to know which device you'll be streaming our services on to give you the best assistance.

MAC Address

The MAC of a "Mag Box" or "OTT IPTV STB" application always starts with: 00:1A:79:... .
The MAC of "Smart IPTV" application is random letters and numbers.

Streaming Line

The streaming line is a m3u URL generated by our server. It's a different way to connect certain devices. The m3u URL can also be in the form of an Xtream Codes API account.

Subscription Period

After studying the best price we can offer to you, we decided as a team to give an option to subscribe with us for a period of 3 months, 6 months, 12 months or 24 months. The default period of a subscription is 24 hours for free!
