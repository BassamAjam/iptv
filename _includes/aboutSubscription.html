Description Of The Fields

Personal Information

The purpose of requesting personal information is to distinguish between our customers and not to make mistakes.

Device

There are many manners to get the IPTV service on your device and it's different between each one. So we always need to know on which device the customer want try our IPTV service to give him the best assistance.

MAC Address

A MAC address is a code that defines certain devices and applications. We usually use it to connect it to our IPTV server. For a device as "Mag Box" and an application as "Stb Emu Pro" or "OTT IPTV STB", their MAC address alway start by 00:1A:79:... . Otherwise, it's a code that contains random letters and numbers.

Streaming Line

The streaming line is a m3u URL generated by our server. It's a different way to connect certain devices. The m3u URL can also be in the form of an Xtream Codes API account.

Subscription Period

After studying the best price we can offer to the customers, we decided as a team to give an option to subscribe with us for a period of 3 months, 6 months, 12 months or 24 months. The default period of a subscription is 24 hours for free!
